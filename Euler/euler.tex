Problem 44.  

Pentagon number.  Given a number, is it a pentagon ?

\begin{flalign}
P_n = \frac{n(3n-1)}{2}, \\
2*P_n = n(3n-1), \\
2*P_n = 3n^2-n, \\
0 = 3n^2-n-2*P_n, \\
\frac{1+\sqrt{1^2 - 4 * 3 * -2*P_n }}{2 * 3}, \\
\frac{1+\sqrt{1^2 + 24P_n }}{6}
\end{flalign}
